Faith Life

As a Christian school, the faith development and character education of our children is devoted to enriching students’ lives and preparing them to lead lives of significance. Lessons on faith and values anchor everyday life. At GTIS, we constantly thank the Lord for the opportunities to serve the students through Education, as well as touch families and the local community with the love and grace of Jesus Christ.

God's Presence in GTIS

Our students begin formal religious studies in Grade 1. They explore a range of topics, from the foundations of Christian theology to the traditions and faiths of other religions. They also learn the value of inclusiveness, as they celebrate the heritage of other faiths.

Students explore these tenets in all their classes, not just in their religious studies. They find the principles meaningful, applicable, and relevant to their daily lives, and we encourage them to discuss their views openly with peers and their teachers.

Students and staff alike are immersed in a Christ-centred setting. Our students are taught Bible stories during assemblies and chapel services, while our staff receive periodical training in spiritual disciplines by pastors and leaders to empower them to mentor and disciple the next generation of God-loving youths.

“... whatever you do, do it all for the glory of God.” — 1 Corinthians 10:31 (NIV)

Service Learning Opportunities

"God is not unjust; he will not forget your work and the love you have shown him as you have helped his people and continue to help them." — Hebrews 6:10 (NIV)

The foundation of our religious programme, and our entire school, is the belief that humanity’s highest good is serving others. Our students participate in a range of community service projects, putting their values into practice while understanding the causes of some of the social injustices they may have the chance to change. Whether they are gathering shoes for cold feet or fasting while feeding the hungry, our students do much more than learn about a subject.

They give themselves and care deeply about others.

School Hymn

Our school hymn embodies the faith we put in God, for He guides and protects us every day. The school hymn is sung twice a day, once before the school day starts, and once at the end of assembly.

Bless our school, dear God we pray
Make it safe by night and day
Bless us with integrity
Keep us in Lord Jesus’ way
Bless the teachers, Lord, we ask
May they find joy in their task
Bless all people who serve this school
May they find Your presence true
May our work be pleasing to You
From now till eternity!
