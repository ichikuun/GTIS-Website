Learning Path at GTIS

The GTIS pedagogy comprises of assemblies, indoor and outdoor learning, sports and Christian education. Dedicated teachers and support staff strive to maximize the learning experience of each child. No effort is spared to inculcate biblical values and a love for lifelong learning.

A typical school day would be as follows:
Time Activity
6.50 – 7.00 am Morning Assembly/Edification
7.00 am – 12.00 pm Morning Session
12.20 – 12.30 pm Afternoon Assembly/Edification
12.30 – 5.20 pm Afternoon Session
12.00 – 1.30 pm Lunch Break
2.45 - 3.30 pm Staff Devotion and Prayer
10.00 am Monthly Chapel Services

Primary Education

The primary years are fundamental to each child’s formative development. At GTIS we are committed to partnering with parents to deliver the best early education experience for your child.

From Grades 1-6, students learn the foundations of core subjects such as English, Mathematics and Science to give them a solid springboard to success later in life. Our highly trained teachers maintain a class ratio of 1:20 to ensure maximum attention is given to every child.

Christian principles are also heavily emphasised during these years. Even in the classroom, Bible stories and characters are weaved into the curriculum to make learning relevant and fun. We make sure that the environment is a safe space for young ones to grow and thrive.

Secondary Education

Students entering their teenage years are full of passion and hunger to learn. Having gained critical thinking and problem-solving skills, 7th to 9th Graders need the right guidance to ensure they mature well.

Subjects such as Social Studies, History and Applied Science are introduced. It is essential for students to explore and think outside the box as they figure solutions to real-world problems. Community work is also progressively introduced to provide students the chance to give back to society.

At the age where students may have questions about faith, life and identity, our devoted staff are on hand to mentor them through. Further spiritual disciplines are taught, empowering each child to know God and share His love with others.

High School

The final stretch of basic education is largely based on getting our students to be future ready. As we play a part in coaching 10th-12th Graders, we are proud of the fine young men and women they are becoming.

Students polish their analytical and synthesis skills through evaluating information in a deep and objective way. Project work and essay writing gives them a taste of the working world to come. Personalised career counselling ensures our students make an informed choice about their next step after graduation.

We take these years to call out the special traits of each one and release them into the areas where they are called. God has a mission for every individual and we journey towards it as a family.

Extra-Curricular Activities

ECAs are a crucial part in our framework for holistic education. Students are highly encouraged to join at least one of our over 50 clubs and societies. By doing so, we develop their interests for non-academic pursuits and increase their involvement in the school community.
