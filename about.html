About Us

The call of God was given in 2005 to: bless, equip and empower Cambodia’s children with quality education in a nurturing environment where Christian values are taught and lived out. To build servant-leaders, pillars of persons and a strategic community for the nation of Cambodia.

Mission

To deliver quality education in a Christ-centred environment, thus
equpping our students spiritually and academically to become leaders with excellent character so as to lead and impact the lives of one another, the
people of Cambodia and beyond.

God-centred
Thinkers,

Servant-Leaders

A GTIS student is always part of the solution. As a forward-looking school, GTIS trains children to blossom into future-ready citizens and contributors
to society.




Motto

Learn. Serve. Lead

Our students live by a passionate pursuit of knowledge and skills. Equipped with a willing heart to perform duties or services for others, they guide and direct others to advance in a meaningful cause.

Values

Resilience — the ability and capacity to recover from difficulties and problems.
Integrity — the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles.
Compassion — a caring, supportive concern for others who are less fortunate.
Excellence — the quality of being outstanding or extremely good.
