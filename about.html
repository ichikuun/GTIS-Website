About Us

The call of God was given in 2005 to: bless, equip and empower Cambodia’s children with quality education in a nurturing environment where Christian values are taught and lived out. We aim to foster servant-leaders, people of fine character and a unified community for the nation of Cambodia.

Mission & Vision

A school’s mission statement describes what a school is seeking to achieve as a community of learners. At GTIS, our mission statement is the cornerstone by which we interact as a community and design strategic plans for the future.

The vision we have that guides the future of each GTIS students is
God-centred
Thinkers,
Intentional Learners, Nation-Builders and
Servant-Leaders

GTIS aims to deliver quality education in a Christ-centred environment, thus equpping our students spiritually and academically to become leaders with excellent character so as to lead and impact the lives of one another, the people of Cambodia and beyond.

Learn. Serve. Lead.

Our motto guides the qualities we impart in every student. We strive to build a community based on Christian teaching and beliefs; encouraging our students to serve and lead as Christ did – to make positive contributions to the world we live in by showing compassion and servant-shaped leadership.

Our students live by a passionate pursuit of knowledge and skills. Equipped with a willing heart to perform duties or services for others, they guide and direct others to advance in a meaningful cause.

R.I.C.E.

Our school values help define and empower us. They contribute to our sense of purpose and vision, and inspire collective action. Our RICE values are:

Resilience — the ability and capacity to recover from difficulties and problems.
Integrity — the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles.
Compassion — a caring, supportive concern for others who are less fortunate.
Excellence — the quality of being outstanding or extremely good.
