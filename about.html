About Us

The call of God was given in 2005 to: Bless, equip and empower Cambodia’s children with quality education in a nurturing environment where Christian values are taught and lived out, building servant-leaders, pillars and a strategic people for the nation of Cambodia.

Mission

To DELIVER QUALITY EDUCATION in a Christ-centred environment, thus EQUIPPING OUR STUDENTS spiritually and academically to become leaders with excellent character so as to LEAD AND IMPACT the lives of one another, the people of Cambodia and beyond.

Vision

God-centred
Thinkers,
Intentional Learners, Nation-Builders and
Servant-Leaders

A GTIS student is not part of the problem, but the solution. As a
forward-looking school, GTIS trains children to blossom into
future-ready citizens and contributors to society.

Motto

Learn. Serve. Lead

Our students live by a passionate pursuit of knowledge and skills, a willing heart to perform duties or services for others and a desire to guide and direct others to advance in a meaningful cause.

Values

Resilience — the ability and capacity to recover from difficulties and problems.
Integrity — the quality of being honest and having strong moral principles.
Compassion
Excellence
