It is my honor to welcome you to Glad Tidings International School. Here, we firmly believe every child deserves an all-rounded education experience that enhances the learning process while teaching them the value of harmonious living.

With each new Academic Year, our enrollment steadily increases as parents become zealous in sending their children to us, in hopes for a brighter future. We are committed to maintaining a close partnership with parents, partner organisations and stakeholders in nurturing children to live by firm Christian values.

Regardless of your role as a parent, donor, volunteer or even a sponsor, join us in our journey to bring hope and transformation to Cambodia through sound teaching and instruction.

Our children are ‘arrows in our quiver’, built up to make a difference in their families and nation.

In Christ,
Jade Sng-Fun Poh Yoke
