Recent Happenings

Mobile Dental Clinic Stops At GTIS

14 January 2018

Throughout the week, primary school students were given priority to access the clinic class by class to get their oral health checked. It was the first time a mobile dental bus made its way into GTIS. Some students were fearful about their experience but soon found that it is a joy to get their teeth cleaned. In addition, dentists gave talks to the student population during assembly in an effort to educate students on the importance of brushing and flossing. Each student was also given a dental kit courtesy of the Ministry of Health.

Chbar mon choir competition

27 November 2017

We are proud to announce that our Secondary School’s Choir came in 2nd Runner-Up at the citywide Chbar Mon Choir Competition three weeks ago. Performing a string of classical songs and even an original piece, they managed to wow the judges by the blend of voices in the Choir. Their hard work paid off after practicing up to three times a week in the months leading up to the competition. Instructor Ms Vang Nukry was extremely pleased with their performance, considering they were not in the top 5 the previous year.

5th Student Council Elections

11 September 2017

Each year, GTIS comes together to elect 20 of its finest students into the Student Council, which serves the school and models exemplary behaviour and conduct. The 5th Student Council will be elected next week. Grade 3-10 council applicants have already wrapped up their council campaign. The school has the cooling-off period to think through their decision. Note that students and staff will have one vote each to be cast into the ballot box. Further details on the election process will be released next Monday during assembly.

Staff Retreat

1 September 2017

Our Staff received a spiritual training programme planned by Ps Richard Toh at the 3-day retreat. Guest speaker Mr Tony Notts from Australia preached the keynote sermon on effective discipleship. Through group discussions and workshops, teachers and administrators alike reflected on their performance for the past year and set goals for the upcoming year. They also had the chance to bond over a BBQ at the seaside. It was a treasured opportunity for all to share in each other’s journey and celebrate the lives of each other.
