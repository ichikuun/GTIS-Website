Calendar

GTIS Family Day & Fair

every March

Secondary and High School students showcase their wide array of talents as they organise a fair for the community every year. Parents, staff and students alike all chip in to raise funds for the school. Highlights include open mic sessions, finger food sales and games stations. At the end of the day each year, the school comes together to form a giant ‘GTIS’ human logo at the field, posing for a photo that will be placed in the year’s yearbook. Everyone has a good day forging stronger ties with each other.

Grade 10 Outdoor Adventure Camp

every May

Camp Radiate, the annual camp for 10th Graders kicked off in the outskirts of Kampong Speu. New and improved activities include the introduction of rafting activities and rappelling at the renovated facility. Students learnt the value of resilience as they braved the elements and various team-building challenges thrown at them during the 4D3N camp. After this valuable experience, each one emerged stronger and more empowered to be a confident leader, friend and individual.

Annual Sports Day 2018

every July

The Annual Sports Day saw staff and students competing in ten competition categories, from basketball to sepak takraw. The Sports Day kicked off with our Board Advisor Pastor Richard Toh saying an opening prayer to bless the competitors. Following which, the entire school engaged in warm-up aerobics led by our Acting Principal Mdm Mryrna. Indeed, we are grateful to God for yet another successful Sports Day that was a great chance for cohesion among the GTIS family.

Cambodian Autumn Festival

every November

Parents and guardians of our students are invited to celebrate the Full Moon Festival with the students every year. As teachers and parents mingle, students can play traditional games and feast on the moon cakes. Everyone is treated to a wide variety of performances by our talented students from the Performing Arts division. Primary school students put up a play, the Secondary school choir sings a medley of Cambodian folk songs and the High school band plays worship songs to end the night.
